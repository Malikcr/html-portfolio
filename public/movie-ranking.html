THE BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO MALI

My top 3 movies or series of all-time

1. ONE PIECE

This is my very favourite Manga and Anime. The Story, the charakters, the animation of the series, everything of it makes me have goosebumps.

2. JOHN WICK

John Wick is my favourite Hollywood Movie. I like the scenes in which John Wick seems to be a invincible enemy for his opponents. I love it how he gets angry, because someone killed his dog and stealed his car. In the first Movie he gets through his enemies until the last one was defeated.

3. RAB NE BANA DI JODI

This movie is my favourite Bollywood Movie. I am a fan of Shah Rukh Khan. I like his artist skills, and how he put emotions in very different and difficult scenes. In this movie he puts so much emotions in his role as a office guy called Surinda Sahni. This is a little different to my other favourites, because here isn't any action in this movie. I just love it because of the emotions that are conveyed to the viewer of this movie.
